who's organizing this?

  • hackathons.pt
  • colégio atlântico
  • ca hack club

hackathons.pt is our ticketing provider/middleman and the main organizer of this hackathon. we're also a 501(c)(3) fiscally sponsored non-profit.

ca hack club is a coding club inside colégio atlântico where we just build cool stuff and organize hackathons. we organized Scrapyard Lisbon, another 24h hackathon a while back and it was extremely fun. we were listed on the news, and are also a fiscally sponsored 501(c)(3) nonprofit.

what is a hackathon, and how will it help them?

hackathons are basically coding competitions where we learn and have fun at the same time. i know, some people rarely believe this can happen, but the places i've learnt the most are hackathons.

yes! we will have a teacher for both days of the hackathon!

since your kid is a student - we have all that info on file!

how much does this cost?

nothing! if you're interested in donating to our cause, we'd really love that. we're a non-profit ran by teens, so we rely on donations by people like you.
